A Letter From Our Vice-Chancellor

Dear Parents and Guardians,

Your trust is everything to us.

When you share your family's information with Redeemer's University, we protect it like our own. Your child's records, your contact details, and personal information are secured behind multiple layers of protection.

Our promise is simple:

› We only collect what's essential for your child's education and safety

› We will never sell or misuse your information

› You control your data and can request changes anytime

› We work only with trusted partners who meet our strict privacy standards

Your family's privacy isn't just our legal duty, it's personal. If you ever have concerns, send a message directly.

Professor Shadrach Akindele

Vice-Chancellor, Redeemer's University

Collaborations & Events

8th NADPA-RAPDP Conference & Annual General Meeting 2025

The 8th NADPA-RAPDP Conference & Annual General Meeting 2025 brings together data protection authorities and experts from across Africa to discuss emerging trends, challenges, and strategies for advancing privacy and data governance on the continent.

1 / 3

MOU signing with the DPCO, TNL Legals

A formal event marking the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding between Redeemer's University and the DPCO - TNL Legals to enhance collaboration on data protection initiatives, training, and legal support as recommended by the DPO.

1 / 3

Data Protection Officer Training

The Data Protection Officer Training was a capacity-building event designed to enhance the knowledge and skills of professionals responsible for data protection compliance. It brought together participants from various sectors to deepen their understanding of data protection laws, responsibilities of DPOs, and practical approaches to managing data privacy risks.
