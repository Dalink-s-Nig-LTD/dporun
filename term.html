Cookies Policy

Understanding how we use cookies to enhance your experience on our website

Last Updated: August 27, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

Our Commitment: We use cookies responsibly and transparently. You have full control over which cookies you accept, and we never use cookies to collect personally identifiable information without your explicit consent.

Types of Cookies We Use

E

Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.

A

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website to improve user experience.

F

Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences and settings to provide enhanced features.

M

Marketing Cookies

Used to show relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness.

Detailed Cookie Categories:

1. Essential Cookies (Always Active)

  • Session Management: Maintain your login status and remember your session
  • Security: Protect against cross-site request forgery and other security threats
  • Load Balancing: Ensure optimal website performance across our servers
  • Cookie Consent: Remember your cookie preferences

2. Analytics Cookies (Optional)

  • Google Analytics: Track website usage, popular pages, and user behavior patterns
  • Performance Monitoring: Identify and resolve technical issues
  • User Journey Tracking: Understand how users navigate through our site
  • Bounce Rate Analysis: Improve content relevance and engagement

3. Functional Cookies (Optional)

  • Language Preferences: Remember your chosen language settings
  • Theme Settings: Maintain your preferred display options
  • Form Data: Save partially completed forms to prevent data loss
  • Accessibility Features: Remember accessibility preferences like font size

4. Marketing Cookies (Optional)

  • Social Media Integration: Enable sharing on social platforms
  • Recruitment Campaigns: Track effectiveness of our student recruitment efforts
  • Event Promotion: Measure engagement with university events and programs
  • Alumni Outreach: Support targeted communication with our alumni network

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner at the bottom of your screen. You can choose to accept all cookies, decline optional cookies, or customize your preferences.

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Cookie Retention

Different types of cookies are stored for different periods:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Essential Functional Cookies: Stored for up to 1 year
  • Analytics Cookies: Stored for up to 2 years
  • Marketing Cookies: Stored for up to 13 months
  • Preference Cookies: Stored for up to 2 years or until you change them
We regularly review and clean up stored cookies to ensure we only keep what's necessary for providing our services.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

While you can browse our website with cookies disabled, some features may not work optimally:

  • You may need to re-enter login information on each visit
  • Your preferences (language, theme) won't be remembered
  • Some interactive features may not function properly
  • We won't be able to provide personalized content recommendations
  • Form data may be lost if you navigate away from a page

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Notify users through our website banner or email
  • Update the "Last Modified" date at the top of this policy
  • Provide a summary of key changes
  • Request fresh consent for any new cookie categories

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding cookies:

  • Right to Consent: Choose which cookies to accept
  • Right to Withdraw: Change your cookie preferences at any time
  • Right to Information: Understand what cookies we use and why
  • Right to Access: Request information about cookies stored on your device
  • Right to Deletion: Request removal of non-essential cookies
